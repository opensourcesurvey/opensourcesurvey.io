The Open Source Survey

Take a 3 minute survey and help identify priorities and challenges of the open-source community.

1. Do you own a GitHub account?

2. What is your involvement with open-source?

Check all that apply

3. How often do you work on open-source/personal projects?

4. Ideally, how much time would you want to work each week on open-source/personal projects?

5. How are your current open-source/personal projects funded?

Check all that apply

6. Have you recently abandoned or neglected a personal project due to a lack of time and/or funding?

7. [OPTIONAL] What is your GitHub username?

8. [OPTIONAL] What is your email address?
